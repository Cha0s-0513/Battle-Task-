# Privacy Policy for Battle Task

**Effective Date: December 26, 2025**  
**Last Updated: December 26, 2025**

## Introduction

Battle Task ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our gamified productivity tracking application.

## Information Collection and Storage

### Data Stored Locally

Battle Task stores all user data **locally on your device** using encrypted storage (FlutterSecureStorage). The following information is stored:

#### Personal Data
- User profile information (name, preferences)
- Battle path selection (Loyalist or Chaos)
- Rank and level progression
- Equipment inventory and discovered items

#### Task and Goal Data
- Tasks created by you (title, type, frequency, completion status)
- Personal and professional goals
- Goal tags and categories
- Project associations
- Document descriptions
- Task completion history and streaks

#### Battle Data
- Battle archives (complete history of all battles)
- Win/loss/draw statistics
- Battle action logs
- Opponent evolution data
- AI learning data (opponent behavior patterns)
- Equipment used in battles
- Weekly battle scores and contributions

#### App Usage Data
- Notification preferences
- App settings and configurations
- Theme preferences
- Last app access timestamps

### Data NOT Collected

We do **NOT** collect, transmit, or store:
- Personal identifying information beyond what you enter
- Location data
- Contact lists
- Camera or microphone access
- Network activity
- Browsing history
- Data from other apps
- Analytics or tracking data
- Advertising identifiers

## Data Transmission

**Battle Task operates entirely offline.** No data is transmitted to external servers, third-party services, or our developers. All processing occurs locally on your device.

### No Internet Connection Required
- The app functions completely offline
- No server communication
- No cloud synchronization
- No data sharing with third parties
- No analytics tracking

## Data Security

### Encryption
All sensitive data is stored using FlutterSecureStorage, which provides:
- AES encryption on Android
- Keychain encryption on iOS
- Secure local storage
- Protection against unauthorized access

### Access Control
- Data is only accessible within the Battle Task app
- No third-party access
- No cross-app data sharing
- Secure storage protected by device security measures

## Data Usage

Your data is used **exclusively** for:
- Task and goal tracking functionality
- Battle system mechanics and calculations
- AI opponent behavior and learning
- Progress statistics and visualizations
- Notification delivery
- App personalization and settings

## Data Retention

### User Control
- Data persists on your device until you delete it
- You can clear specific data through app settings
- Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently
- No backup copies are created outside your device

### Automatic Deletion
- Battle archives maintain maximum 100 most recent battles
- Old battle records are automatically removed when limit is exceeded
- No other automatic data deletion occurs

## Third-Party Services

Battle Task does **NOT** use:
- Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services
- Crash reporting tools
- Third-party authentication
- Payment processors
- Location services

## Children's Privacy

Battle Task does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and can be used by anyone. Parents should review this policy and supervise their children's use of the app.
